## What is AdsQr?

**AdsQr** is a performance engine for real-world advertising. Unlike AdBazaar (which books ad inventory), AdsQr is for **instant campaign deployment** — a poster, table stand, product label, event banner, anywhere.

**Core Purpose:** Generate QR codes → Drive real-world engagement → Track scan → visit → purchase → Measure ROI

## The Multi-Step Conversion Funnel

### Why This Matters

```
WITHOUT MULTI-STEP:
├── Users farm coins (scan & forget)
├── No real business impact
└── Merchants lose faith

WITH MULTI-STEP:
├── Scan: low reward (acquire attention)
├── Visit: medium reward (drive action)
├── Purchase: high reward (generate revenue)
└── Repeat: brand coins (build loyalty)
```

### Hybrid Coin System

| Layer | Coin Type | Purpose | Where Spent |
|-------|-----------|---------|-------------|
| **Acquisition** | REZ Coins | Universal rewards | Anywhere in ReZ |
| **Retention** | Brand Coins | Repeat visits | Only at that merchant |

### Pricing Model

```
PRIMARY: Coin-based billing

Brand buys ₹10,000 worth of coins
├── System distributes coins per action
├── You keep ₹1,000-2,000 (10-20%)
└── Users receive ₹8,000-9,000 in coins

OPTIONAL: Minimum campaign fee
└── ₹99/month base fee (covers platform costs)
```
