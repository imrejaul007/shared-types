# ReZ Ride — Product Concept

## Vision

ReZ Ride transforms the ride-hailing model by replacing driver commissions with an advertising-based revenue engine. Drivers earn more, users save money, and advertisers reach a captive, intent-qualified audience.

---

## Business Model

### The Problem

Traditional ride-hailing platforms (Uber, Ola, Rapido) charge drivers 20-25% commission on every ride. This:

- Reduces driver earnings by 20-25%
- Forces drivers to work longer hours
- Creates tension between platform and drivers
- Drivers may cut corners on maintenance/safety

### The Solution

ReZ Ride eliminates commission entirely and funds operations through in-vehicle advertising:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ REVENUE FLOW │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ │
│ Advertiser pays CPM (Cost Per Mille) │

│ │ │
│ ▼ │
│ ReZ Platform (30%) │
│ • Platform operations │
│ • Ad serving infrastructure │
│ • Customer acquisition │
│ │ │

│ │ │
│ ├── 60% → Driver │
│ │ • Extra income │

│ │

```

## The Screen as Revenue Center

### Screen Value Proposition for Drivers

| Without Screen | With Screen |
|----------------|-------------|
| 100% fare | 100% fare |
| No extra income | Ad revenue share |
| Platform takes | Platform enables |
|  | Screen subsidy (optional) |

### Screen Revenue Model

```
Assuming 10 rides/day × 30 days = 300 rides/month
Average ride: 20 minutes
Total screen time: 6,000 minutes = 100 hours

At 50% ad fill rate and ₹5 CPM:
100 hours × 50 ads/hour × 50% fill = 2,500 impressions
2,500 impressions ÷ 1,000 × ₹5 = ₹12.50 ad revenue/month

Driver share (60%): ₹7.50/month
Platform share (40%): ₹5.00/month

Scale with higher fill rate:
80% fill @ ₹10 CPM = ₹60/month to driver
```

### Screen Hardware Program Options

| Model | Who Pays | Pros | Cons |
|-------|----------|------|------|
| **A: ReZ Provides** | ReZ (CAPEX) | Fast deployment | Risk if drivers churn |
| **B: Driver Buys** | Driver (OPEX) | No CAPEX risk | Slower adoption |
| **C: Subscription** | Driver monthly | Cash flow predictable | Barrier to entry |
| **D: Hybrid** | Initial ReZ, buyout option | Flexibility | Complexity |

**Recommended:** Model A initially → transition to Model B as trust builds

| Competition | Move fast, build ecosystem lock-in first |
| Intent data gaps | Incentivize users to use more ReZ services |
